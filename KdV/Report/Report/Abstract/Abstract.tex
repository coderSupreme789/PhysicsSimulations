\section*{Abstract}\label{sec:abstract}

\tbd

In this report, we investigate the single and double soliton solutions to the Korteweg-De Vries equation. The Korteweg-De Vries equation is used to model shallow water waves. The form we are using is in the dimensionless form as presented in Section \ref{sec:intro}.

We also investigate the motion of soliton solutions when periodic boundary conditions are used as described in the method.
